Welcome to Tsunada's Lab!

Tsunada Lab aims to understand:

1) how primates flexibly control and adapt their vocal behaviors during social communication and

2) how the brain circuitry performs the computations that mediate this ability.

We address these questions by applying neurophysiological techniques to freely-communicating marmoset monkeys. We hope our studies for vocal communication in normal subjects will contribute to a better understanding of how and why many patients with mental or developmental disorders have problems in vocal communication.
